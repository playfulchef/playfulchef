About Playful Chef

Playful Chef was founded in 2005 in San Francisco, CA by Lisa Ligon, a former high tech salesperson who decided that pursuing the mission of Playful Chef was much more compelling than selling semiconductors. The idea for developing kid’s cooking kits that incorporate life learning lessons came from teaching culinary education classes as a volunteer to 100+ students. It was an incredibly rewarding experience that sparked the passion and creativity needed to create a phenomenal line of kid’s cooking products.

Playful Chef’s mission is to provide an integrated educational culinary experience that supports parent’s aspirations to teach their kids invaluable cooking skills and healthy eating habits that will last a lifetime.

Why Playful Chef?

The kitchen is the new playground. Our healthy and tasty recipes are a fun and rewarding way to teach your kids important life skills:

Playful family time

Siblings, parents, aunts, uncles, and grandparents alike can connect through cooking.

Sharing is caring

Food tastes more delicious when shared.

Following directions

Your kids will learn how to easily measure ingredients using color coded cups and spoons and follow step-by-step instructions to cook only healthy and tasty food.

Math and science

Introduce your kids to basic fractions as they measure and mix ingredients. Cooking ingredients is a tangible demonstration for your kids to learn basic science concepts.

Kitchen safety

Teach your kids what’s OK and NOT OK to touch in the “kid area” of your kitchen.
